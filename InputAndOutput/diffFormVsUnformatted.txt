* Formatted I/O
1. Control strings are used.
2. printf() and scanf() are the functions for Formatted I/O.
3. Example 
    int a,b;
    scanf("%d%d",&a,&b);
    printf("a=%d, b=%d", a,b);


* Unformatted I/O
1. Control strings aren't used.
2. getchar(),putchar(),gets() and puts are some functions used for Unformatted I/O.
3. Example
    char ch;
    ch=getchar();
    putchar(ch);

    char str[20];
    gets(str);
    puts(str);
